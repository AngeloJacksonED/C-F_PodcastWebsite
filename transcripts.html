Transcripts

We provide podcast scripts that we create from time to time.

This is useful for anyone requiring closed captions or is hard of hearing.

Note: Not all episodes we make get a transcript, if you like to help us make one, download a copy of our episode, then analyze our episode and begin writting. Audio to Text providers are also fine to use. Contact us by email or on our Twitter handle if you would like to send us a copy of the transcript so we can put it on the website! We will credit you. Thanks for contributing!

More episodes will be added here every once a while.
