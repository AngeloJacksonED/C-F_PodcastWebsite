BREAKING - Plushy Camel's Neck Got Ripped Open!

By camelfrogadmin - April 9th, 2021.


Whoops! A Mishap that turned down-right wrong!

An image of the Ripped Stuffed Neck.

Occurred on 4/8/21 - Reported on 4/9/21.

Note for all: No Real Animals or Camels were harmed. This is just a Plushy Toy that got Ripped.

So. Yesterday Evening, Camel got his Neck ripped open. This happened when one of our staff members tried to grab Camel but the Neck got pulled open and released his Fluff. Once that happened, a complete moment of shock and silence happened.

This is one of the rarest moments that a stuffed toy gets ripped, we have seen stuffed toys get ripped unexpectedly and a hard pull can cause it! As people would say you MUST take care of your stuff! Another incident occurred when Camel's top neck was opened and then repaired with Black String. Camel was then cleared for use. Now on 4/8/21, it got even worse. The Rip is just below the bottom Neck area of the Plushy Camel. Yet Another Incident occurred when another Camel's rip got so serious, that Camel got to be removed. This feels like a story that will never end!

Mr B.F.F said in a reaction post when staff shared him the news: "That is sooo shocking! Oh my god!".

Angelo also had to add on this: "Oh No! This is so saddening. Hope this gets fixed."

A close by viewer also reacted to this.... "This is just like the Woody who got his arm ripped in Toy Story 2!" So saddening for the Camel especially!

The Good News is that Camel will have Episode 8 of his Podcast ready to be released. We have no word whether or not Episode 9 will feature Frog himself with the Guest Caller Robot Jr, coming up to be recorded and produced. Robot Jr is also notified of the news.

So while we are stuck in the middle of a weekend and that the crew must stay at home, we don't have a person to sew it up. Andrew (who was contacted at the matter) will be in touch with a friend of his that will hopefully fix things up for Camel so he can get back into production and picture taking if needed. Although Andrew is on break, the news sent to him shocked him and will monitor this situation for now. Mr B.F.F tried asking his friend to do it but he couldn't do it. He has no interest. He asked another friend who was into sewing but sadly, he is in the midst of school work, so he respected that.

AngeloJacksonED was also shocked to see this. Although he is also busy on work, he will be unable to assist as of right now, we know that he will return to fix things up with Camel once he gets back.

While it isn't too serious, Frog will be stationed to film episode 9 in the coming days. Episode 8 will be released soon enough. Frog said in a statement: "It's very shocking to see my friend, Camel in a position like this, we have seen so many stuffed toys get ripped unexpectedly these days and we show sympathy for Plushy Camel and all the best into getting him fixed." The update for Camel, he is now relaxing but still needs to be sewed. The plan is for the crew to post his episode 8 ASAP. Episode 9 will be ready in the works as the promotion swap must come in by one month! We also want Camel to still have is neck still go up, not down.... when it comes to taking nice pictures and videos of him. Let's just hope nothing fall down again, as they thought.

Conclusion:

Will Camel get fixed before Episode 9 or 10 gets finished? We will find out soon enough but please vote using this Interactive Poll and test your minds to a prediction that may turn out to be the real event! We wish Camel nothing but the best of luck. We will be providing necessary updates to share with you how Camel is doing, right now, no status of him be sewed right now but he will be accommodated to get fixed in the coming days! Stay tuned.
