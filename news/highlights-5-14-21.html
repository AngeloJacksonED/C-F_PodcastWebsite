The Second Highlights & Recap: Episode 6-10

By AngeloJacksonED - May 14th, 2021.


Let's view the last 5 episodes!

Time for a recap!

Hello! We are back to the Highlights and our own Behind the Scenes Scrapbook. Make sure to read the other articles and watch Episodes 9 and 10 to get the latest scoops on what has happened for the past 4 weeks.

Otherwise, enjoy the article!

Episode 6:

Episode 6 was a slight bum to the list but without Frog, things really go much smoother. However, ratings lagged a bit and plays to the streams dropped a bit. Overall, Robot 2 goes over his story on his house which foreshadows a expensive plan. Not real! He’s imagining. - “Plus, half our net income???”

And finally this episode mentions a “Green Butterfly”. A friend who thinks a green butterfly believes the story may be a hoax! We added the story if viewers had any opinion. Our friend still had the same memory that there was a green butterfly in the Christmas Convention Center back then. We are looking at situation off-studio/off-site as well. Angelo said: “We are looking into Mr B.F.F’s thoughts since he knows his past on this more than we do.” Mr B.F.F said, “I have no idea”. Others were not able to comment.

Episode 7:

One of our superior episodes to have an interesting opening, after watching a gameplay of Formula 1. In this usual episode, Angelo makes a cameo appearance, thanks to him for being featured. Our friend Mr B.F.F who joined our podcast, told what he was doing on his YouTube channel, doing gaming and more! He also introduced his story on the GoodJobBoy760000 Fund. Great to see it alive and make sure to check our Mr B.F.F’s story oh his website! It features an end to his podcast-era and now, he’s moving on from it. https://bit.ly/3fkrixN"

The team really liked the episode and we wish him the best of luck now as he helped us worked on the last set of episodes that he pushed and it came in successfully. Thus episode 7 has average ratings. We sent Mr B.F.F a studio master tape/file after his appearance to show our appreciation.

Episode 8:

This episode was recorded well in advance as things goes up and up. “Plushy Camel” (which we had to name it for detection reasons) had his neck broken and still needs to be sewed. The fluff is getting worse and our team is still waiting. Life is pushing our staff harder even in these times right now.

Episode 8 was prepared with a dispatch game ad. It was brought up to our attention when our staff was watching a YouTube video then realized the ad was playing on his phone. Then he told the others that was one cringy video and it was funny! It triggered us to add it for our review, eventually it would make it into two episodes because it even appeased Mr B.F.F so much, even at the night! Ratings for the episode went down a bit but did bounce back because of the audio clips in there. But anyways, it was added. Oh and….. “you are nine hundred, eleven %!” Try again. We stopped showing it after another ad show, it was getting bad and it was worse for wear now, if you know what we mean. We’ll visit it sometime. Here’s the link of the mobile app. https://bit.ly/2Qi0aHa"

The segment also contains audio captcha’s you could find anywhere. The one’s we’ve pulled out were from Amazon.

Hey! Didn’t we forget to mention, we are on Amazon Music! Find us here.

Episode 8 also featured Butterfly and his Good Society story, we are still supporting it today as well! We now wish him the best of luck too!

Episode 9:

Episode 9 with the nice calm, non-cheesy theme comes after Plushy Camel gets some rest out of the studio. Frog goes with Robot Jr. W ith his Robot Fund story. It revisits the dispatch game and they fill up with helium. Also we went for McDonalds for a burger with no onions, lol! After “a few moments later”, Frog and Robot emulates the ad with helium voices, the audience hated it lol! “Wut” - Episode 9 and 6.

We still continue to see our friends of the Robot Fund who are doing a great job. Good luck Robot Fund!

Episode 10:

To wrap up the recap, episode 10 looks at optical illusions and WoF fails. “Double Digits Already.” Nothing else that is interesting except Macaroni telling his story which was very interesting to hear. In any other case, go visit MacaroniMii on Twitter. Congratulations on a successful run as Macaroni is really doing it well. https://bit.ly/2RjUFbA"

Our promotion swap guests (with agreements to share our trailers) ended up being this particular podcast, Go check them out! https://bit.ly/33HEpDM"

Conclusion:

And there you have it! Episodes 11 and 12 are to be made and produced for you now. Hope you enjoyed the last 5 episodes! This also marks the end of Mr B.F.F’s reign for sure! Also if you want to be a guest, find us on Audry. Have a great night everyone!
