What's the transition?

Things escalated very quickly, voices in, editing in and another script in. Angelo saying, "This is some serious work in mind, we gotta make sure things go in as fast as we can.... Thanks to Mr B.F.F's speedy work, and some bumps along the way, we are almost in the completed area of success and trust. Now that we are in the double digits now, we should step it up and make something more original as soon as possible without all of the fluster.

As mentioned in the quote as promised, and in the main podcast description, more entertainment stories will be share and Frog along with the caller's opinions will be a good fit for episode 10 and above.

While Episode 10 is still in the works...... a friend of our's sent in a script of episode 11, we liked it and sent it to our staff so we can evaluate it! We thanked the creator as well. Staff has plans for episode 11 and 12 to work on the podcast protocol's main scripts though and if it goes in, there will be changes to fit in the next slot of episodes, especially if Camel ever gets fixed soon.

What's the update on Camel?

So far, Camel has not yet gone into a procedure of stitching just yet, Andrew got in touch with a friend who will fix him. We received lots of potential emails about Camel being fixed but unfortunately we've turned them down. So we do not need a second man for this now.... We, the staff, Frog, Macaroni and Mr B.F.F gives good wishes for Camel. Here we received a message from Camel.

"Hey guys. Camel here. I hope you are doing well. Especially for me, I am at home with loved ones in hoping to recover very soon. I got the text from Andrew that said someone will help me get fixed. I am excited and hopefully even more when I get back in the studio when I return but for now. Please care for me and your friends and loved ones as well and I will see you all soon back at the podcast as soon as possible. Ciao!" - Camel.

To wrap up this section, the staff will be ready for whatever changes it will have to go through, if it's big or small, a challenge really steps up our competition and we're loving it so far.

Conclusion - Final Words

So far, we just published episode 9 a few days ago so if you want, check it out! We are looking forward for an interesting 2 more weeks ahead. Have an awesome start to this new week!
