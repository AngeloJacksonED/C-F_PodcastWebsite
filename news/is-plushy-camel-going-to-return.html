Is Plushy Camel Going to Return? - More Highlights!

By AngeloJacksonED - June 23th, 2021.



Plushy Camel coming back? Finale Updates? Tell us!

A Wild Poster to this Article.

With just one episode left and Episode 11 released, the finale is about to be near and dear as Frog might expect a friend of his and he has planned it on the Finale. The finale episode is almost done and is planned for July 17th. To catch up on things, watch our previous episodes and look at older articles to see what’s going on right now. In the meanwhile for the rest of you people, Stay tuned for Episode 12! In the meanwhile here is some more news.

When will Camel return?

We have no updates but on a episode, we will give something special to share in the finale. Camel is in the Repair room right now but not fixed yet. No update whether he will be sewed but we will try to add him back somehow in Series 2. We apologize if it’s taking a while because the staff and friends here are quite busy this year.

What will happen to the podcast in the near future?

Since a hard week is about to come for us, we’ll be a little busy to get through our podcast and contact might be limited since we are doing Summer Paperwork and it’s a no guarantee if there is going to be more work for us to make. If for some reason, we can’t go on the internet, we will be able to share our episode automatically scheduled. Our website’s release may be delayed. The master copies are stored on our website. While we do our best to filter the traffic and plays on our website, we will be implementing tools to help improve the user experience on the listen page, we might migrate from HTML5 players in compatible MP3 plugins so we can track the number of plays on our website. Also our website may have some changes on it’s way too. Also, the staff has been vaccinated as well to be safe! So anyways, just keep fingers crossed for our staff to survive a tough week and get back to the final touches.

What will happen after the finale?

After final touches, we will continue to update our website, mainly update the listen and about pages to fit series 1, 2 and 3 and also “Frog Does It All”. We might build a more dedicated website to it’s own podcast but however we wanted to merge both titles in one domain so we don’t need to buy and waste money. Only if we built new podcasts, staff is interested in….. We can also share news articles on this website as possible every once a while to stay active until September, since COVID protocols are being lifted and as we can safely work in life, our time in the Podcast can be very limited. We are going back to work, full time. And as time goes by, we need 1 more year before we have more independent time. That’s all the staff can share for us.

What will we expect for Series 2?

After a successful “Frog Does It All” Episode and one that is exceeding 5 - 5 & 1/2 minutes , our goal will be continuing on the episodes length and clarity. We have a pretty good format and adding more topics. Unique topics and also be converted to news articles to help us grow reach and traffic possibly. No promises that this can become successful but we hope that we will continue to stride and add new experiences in the Podcast.

Can you give us a Sneak Peak in the Finale?

We are almost done in production and a special guest has come to take the Floor! You will see it on it’s release date due by July 17th. Trying to add more length and pieces from old episodes. Which we can elaborate and build on as well, Simply put. We are deciding if we need to finish now or later because things can stack up before the episode editing is done or after the release date is due, we might delay it. Who knows? We never thought it be harder like ever before, not that this is the first time.

In Conclusion:

We can finally settle things down and focus on one task in hand after we get episode 12 live. From experience overall in Series 1, there were some ups and downs. We plan to have Series 2 better than it was. Angelo is ready to take it on. And why does it say Series 3 in the home page you ask? Series 1 was conducted by the Former Mr B.F.F running 5 episodes. Series 2 with Angelo had 7 episodes, and Series 3 will have more! Get ready for what is coming to happen! More updates in the future, be on the lookout folks.
