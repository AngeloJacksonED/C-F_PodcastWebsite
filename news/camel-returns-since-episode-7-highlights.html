Camel Returns Since Episode 7! Highlights

By AngeloJacksonED - July 12th, 2021.



Notice: Spoilers might be mentioned. If you haven’t listened to the Episodes corresponding to the article, We suggest you listen to it now before reading. (12)

Camel Returns for the Finale (episode 12) on the Set.

Camel is also known as Plushy Camel since we can’t name his Camel for mentioning purposes.

Hiya! Episode 12 is up now! Check out the Season finale. Available on both the Mobile app, our website and where you listen to your favorite podcasts. Staff was over-joyed by how the outcome went and it just had to end to perfection. Andrew saying, “Welp, that’s a wrap! Hope everybody enjoyed themselves and we are looking forward to have our staff come for Series 3!” Series 3 goals are to be worked on. Series 1 was first developed by Mr B.F.F for 5 episodes, 7 episodes on Series 2, created by Angelo and Series 3 will be much longer! We can’t believe we are only starting this strong!

Here's the story.

For the first time since the transition from Frog Does It All including the 2nd version, the time when the host and co-hosts arrived, Plushy Camel makes a return to the studio ever since he’s been away, since it was the finale and Camel felt happy to return for a day! Staff thought they take it lightly and have Camel on-board. Camel saying in a small statement, “A great honor to return and say a few things as well as looking at the fun memories, me and Frog did. It was enjoyable. Until then, I shall get fixed. Frog saying, “It’s the finale anyways, who would of thought, I would have chosen Plushy Camel as well!”. Plushy Camel agreeing on his statement as a response back.

Frog is always a close friend to Plushy Camel and would consider for another episode as Guest Caller and even moderating the Podcast however, as much as we enjoyed his older episodes, we will wish him all the best to be fixed and get back into the groove soon enough. Just looking at the files made Camel hyped including the Episode yesterday which he enjoyed. Plushy Camel listened to the older episode highlights and it just had to make him cry a little……. When Camel left the Studio at Robot 2’s hallway, he said, “It was cool, amazing and fun, simply put”. Then the studio was closed down for the day.

And this means, our Series comes to a close for now.

Don’t leave just yet, in the coming days, we will be upgrading the site to enhance our next Series. Since we can’t always have an interactive poll built in, we’ll be adopting the Q&A page. Since an increase of user messages and emails that came in, there will be a place to show the answers from the previous Q&A’s Frog made for you! Those will be coming soon along with other updates. If you find a loophole or a bug in the site, you can always email us or tweet us. Our email is listed in our App’s store listing.

We’ll also be building and writing more articles for you as well, there’s probably nothing else we can write at the moment, you can also submit an idea article and that could appear on the News Page. Also with September coming, most of us are working again Full-time so we might not fit in some free-time to build more episodes but we’ll see what we can come up with during our time.

Well, if that’s all to say, then have a great summer and see you all for Series 3 coming in September or October! Peace out, y’all!
