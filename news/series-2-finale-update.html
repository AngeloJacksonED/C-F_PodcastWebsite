Episode 12 Marks the End of Series 2

By froggyhaul (Admin) - May 10th, 2021.


End of a series/season, still yet to come....

A banner simply named "the finale teaser."

Hey guys. We are writing this story to give you impressive updates on what happened. Watch episode 9 to catch up! Staff has already finished productions for Episode 10 and marks a lot of events that are happening right now and in the future! Also read the other articles to see what's been happening lately as well. Please have a good read through those so you completely understand them, our staff recommends. For now, Let's check out the next set of events to come....

For some this story can be confusing but it's the chemistry that really sets our podcast afloat. Enjoy!

THE WHOLE STORY - part 1.

Now that both Episodes 9 and 10 are finished, staff cheered with joy that this finally came to an end because it brought up lots of hard work for our crew, yet teams were able to prove how great they were into making the final project. While Angelo was there to assist, around 80% were capable of working on the project, Angelo needed for the podcast.

Reason? Because staff can only come by once a month and one episode, once a month. Angelo didn't like the proposal set out because the podcast would've been producing 12 episodes in a year. So there was always a reason why a challenge can be great for all. Angelo's goal is to have more episodes for the next series as the Podcast grows. As for life, Improving Life problems also helps too and that's what Angelo is also working on during these times but yet, "There will always be a good partner to work with." - Angelo.

Special Thanks to Macaroni Mii and Robot Jr. The Last 2 callers who were able to attend and push our podcast forward to success.

THE ENDING OF MR B.F.F's REIGN OF PODCAST WORK - Part 2.

So far so good! So now, after this.... it officially marks the end of Mr B.F.F and his days with us on the podcast he created. He is working and improving on his Mental Health which is going very strong and focuses on one task and tries not to multi-task too much. We respect his decision and hope for the best. Mr B.F.F had to add on his final words.

"I will always be there for everyone on the podcast, No matter what. You guys were so awesome and now I hope you will pass it on for everyone to listen onto. Good luck." - Mr B.F.F.

THE ENDING OF SERIES 2 - Part 3.

The main part of the story here, Episode 12 will officially mark the end of Series 12. The staff have made the decision to close it off because a new series will be planned and if Camel doesn't return on-time, Series 3 will route the same way on how it's going to be planned. We are not announcing specifics at this time but the crew plan to have longer episode lengths, more reviews, and more fun with out the fuzz in hopes for ratings to increase. The final decision of the Series 2 closure was made by Angelo who had ideas in these plans. That means that Angelo and the team might be putting huge and brand new changes to route the Series 3 way and improve ratings and podcast episode that gets rolled out! Angelo said this on his statement.

"Series 3 is in the works, we got approval exclusively by the AndrewED team that the Podcast can keep going. Let's push it." - Angelo.

Andrew added: "This podcast has a long way to go and we are capable of holding it much longer as we can as well. Let's go and do it, y'all!"

Conclusion

While things do come to an end.... we are still continuing on. As they say, "The Show Must Go On." We will stride to take stride on our Podcast and work for Episodes 11 and 12 to end off Series 2. Angelo will be in prep for the unforeseeable future of Series 3. Whether Camel will return or not, planning is of the essence.

Stay tuned for episodes 11 and 12 and check out Episode 10 shortly. Have a great day everyone. We are also on Amazon Music now. Check us out.
