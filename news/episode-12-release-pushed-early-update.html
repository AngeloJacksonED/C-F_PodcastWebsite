Episode 12 is Pushed Early for it's Release

By AngeloJacksonED - July 8th, 2021.



NEW UPDATE: Episode 12 might be released earlier than expected. SCROLL FOR UPCOMING DATE.

Episode 12’s original release was originally set for July 17th.

Hello, I’m here today to write a quick update. We are sharing an update to our finale schedule. This comes after we spoke about how staff and viewers by popular demand wants to close off the studio so we can take a break. Don’t worry, this website won’t be affected, we will continue to update it every once a while. AndrewED will make the changes to the site also added fresh new articles, me, Andrew or my staff can work on. Also that summer’s still here, we’ll be sometimes busy but always open. Also make sure to catch up the previous episodes as well.

Due to popular demand and having meetings by staff, We have ultimately came into agreement that the episode should be set early….. Angelo and friends agreed to make the episode shift early as we want to finish the series and make early changes to the format as soon as possible. Also the staff wants to have a break just so we can improve our health, study and be productive at something else.

In the meanwhile, Angelo is working on a photo set for the “Frog Does It All”’s new upcoming Series and it’s unique banners. As you saw on the last episode, (episode 11) and the upcoming finale episode, the format will remain the same along with former Headmaster Layla and Robot Jr stationed around town. Robot 2 is also being renewed as announcer well, and finally, your’s truly. Frog will be at the studio for new callers. Along the way, we will also have 4 brand new guest callers will stories just like the previous episodes which had 4 special guest callers, (episodes 7-10) in which they’ll be on-board and making a story that we and all can support. If you don’t know how the Guest Caller format works, you are free to read how the first four guest callers took action on the Podcast. No idea on name’s yet but we’ll make it good. Also each episode will have longer length which is good and more entertaining for the Podcast. Series 3 will have more great content so make sure to follow us on the run to new original content as always!

What will Angelo be doing ahead of time?

I’ll be doing work on the photos as well as building more ideas. In late August or September which ever comes first, we’ll be back in the studio for filming and prep. However due to one our main coordinators having a appointment, we might have to delay our work since we are working with him while we go to setup our new episodes. We might have to push back at October if things don’t go to plan. We will have to see where Series 3 will be set for and how the future could be.

After all of that highlights, The finale episode is now set to release on July 11th., 2021. Stay tuned, watch it in our streaming services and our website! Our listen page will be updated for the latest episode airdate and new trailer. Setting the episode earlier is a good move and we can prioritize what the future of our Podcast will be. Oh, and for Camel, it’s a secret….. ahem. Should be good.

See you then.
