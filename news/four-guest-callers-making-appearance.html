4 Upcoming Guest Callers in Action!

By AngeloJacksonED - March 27th, 2021.


As we get more episodes in, we gathered 4 guest callers for those sessions.

Hi. We are compiling more guest callers made of real human figures, fictional characters and creativity into our podcast. Our main purpose, Sharing their story back. We have planned 4 guests hosts already that will fill in portions and episodes of the podcast for 2 minutes or more to the podium.

Director Angelo proactively monitors every caller for time, fairness and clarity.

As well, one of our episodes will be the promotion swap, TBD but we are happy that we are sharing more stories with broader brands and podcasts!

Mr B.F.F - Guest Caller for Episode 7 - YouTuber with 1.45K subs

Mr B.F.F is a gamer and posts his videos to YouTube with over 1,000 subs with a fair 606,717 views, also having a projects channel with miscellaneous content with 250 subs. He says that he is a senior moderator of the speedrun board "Game Party" 1, 2, 3 and In Motion.

He was also our former owner of this podcast and creator of only 4 episodes! We gave him a call and gladly accepted the call to comeback!

His story is the GoodJobBoy760000's Foundation. You will find out that story in the next episode. Stay tuned! FUN FACT: GoodJobBoy760000 is his "NNID" if you know what we mean. Go friend him if you have a chance!

In addition, you can read the previous article on his Q&A Story we've recently posted on his career.

Thoughts on his return:.... "It was such an honor to comeback and talk once more."

___________

Butterfly- Guest Caller for Episode 8 - Previous caller for Episode 5

Butterfly was a guest caller for episode 5 of the "C#5 and their thoughts about the internet #1" episode. Although, Butterfly didn't share his story, He will be sharing "The Good Society" story with us on the podcast.

He was also known for having a friend named "Green Butterfly" in which Camel know that exist but his caller doesn't. We still await for that information soon though!

You can also find a story of Butterfly in the About Page we recently had put up and talked about with Frog and how they share the same traits.

What he had to say: "Thrilling to return for more, feels like I am a asset to the podcast. Thank you."

___________

Robot Jr. - Guest Caller for Episode 9 - Son of Robot 2

Robot Jr. was featured in Frog's Syndicated Millionaire Parody, Camel's short Pizza skit and in Robot's Chain Reaction Parody.

While Robot Jr. was not in any major projects Angelo or Mr B.F.F created, he is being put as a guest caller and will share us a story on The Robot Fund. Sounds cool! We are looking forward to that for the Episode 9!

Overall Feedback on his arrival: "Pleased to work just like my father did, looking forward to seeing my episode air pretty soon."

___________

Macaroni Mii - Guest Caller for Episode 10 - Friend of Mr B.F.F and Sports Club Manager

Macaroni Mii is a Sports Club manager in Vancouver. Follow him on Twitter: @MacaroniMii. He also has accumulated plenty of social media followers and usually reads some books always and is usually pleasent.

Mac's overall opinion: "Will be so fun to see myself on the podcast, can't wait for it!"

His story is on his Sports Club by the way. Check out for that on episode 10!

___________

While these guest callers are sharing their stories, we are on the works on making a new episode pretty soon. AngeloJacksonED and AndrewED will be backup assistants to make sure things go right, they might end up in future guest caller episodes in the future!

In the meanwhile, we want you to keep an eye out for episode 7 of the podcast and share this news story.

Have a great day!
