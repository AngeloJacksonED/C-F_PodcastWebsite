Mr B.F.F - Remembering His Online Memories

By AngeloJacksonED - March 22nd, 2021.


Remember Mr B.F.F, wanted a Q&A from him? Know a little bit of his background!

Hello everyone, It's Spring Season now! We hope you're having a great day. Also hoped you watch episode 6 of the podcast. Quick shout-out to Randal who messaged us on Audry and watched our podcast, I am assuming? Thank you for the feedback.

In the meanwhile, as you may have known, at the very first post, Mr B.F.F has been our creator and director for only 4 episodes of our podcast. While he was working on a great job here on our podcast, we wanted to bring him back for some more!

SPOILER ALERT:

Mr B.F.F will be our guest caller for our next episode!!!

Here are the questions we examined upon entry back the podcast room.

Q: What made you consider doing Camel and Frog overall?

A: YouTube videos. I didn't want my channel to be completely blank, so I made some videos about random stuff on my main channel. When it got evolved over time, it was time just to delete them and find a way of working with creativity, originality and ideas, so we put them on a podcast and let's hope for the good!

Q: How specifically did it evolved as you mentioned?

A: At first, no popularity, me being a smaller creator is like sitting down in paradise for me. As soon as we improve with better equipment, technology, matureness and discipline, it just started to get old too, but still as I liked it, I had ideas for it but as soon as we head to the gaming side of the story, it is soon to be "no more" for now because I just got busy and I couldn't multi-task lots of projects at once.

At 12/21/18, things had a huge turn-around, I immediately got into tech so much it opened my pathway to more product testing and I got into the groove with what I was doing. Simply, the Camel and Frog was to be done for..... until my friend played jokes and tried to pull me into making something with the plush toys again. In the meanwhile, I was just doing my thing until I had a community, 1000 times big. I made friends and they would help me on the prospects of my work as well. I took the opportunity to work with the plushies again.

So far, the videos I made back in 2015 to 2016 started getting dislikes, maybe because of the cringe in the videos? I started to pull down videos in what I tried to do and keep some of the Camel and Frog videos as possible. And that's when I served enough interest to make a podcast. When I got stressed out while making episode for the podcast, one friend immediately took the job and that's you, Angelo!

Q: How did this hobby began?

A: It was a day at the Vancouver Convention Centre where Christmas events were held. We got a chance to see Camel stuffed toys as well as Frogs from next year. Immediately I got into it and started looking into producing something for them. Blah, blah blah, and here we are in the present.

Q: Why are you too stressed out already?

Tons of work-load, pressure got into me, It could've been more stressful.... only if I had more freetime.

Q: What does creativity mean to you?

Making your own ideas without stealing or reusing stuff without a strong original part, like video games may not be original unless you add more background or educational value. Our podcast is simply original, that I can argue about.

Q: What goals have you worked on?

Social skills, having the right amount of communication and memory. Setting a good schedule for the day.

Q: When you will arrive on our episode, what do you expect?

Going with the flow, moving in the right pace, not all nervous and ready to go. I am excited to be back for a day to work with you once more Angelo and Team! See you there folks!

That's pretty much it for him. You will see him in the next episode of our podcast. EPISODE 7. Stay tuned and ring in for the next episode!

If you want to get in touch with Mr B.F.F, you may send him a message on Twitter. @MrBFF5418.

Angelo saying Goodbye for now! See you there on the next episode!
